Client Portal – Target Feature Set
1) Unified Client Portal (core hub)

White-labeled client portal with your branding and custom domain support.

One login to access support, services, reports, training, announcements, and account details.

Mobile-friendly web portal plus optional desktop app experience.

Optional in-app portal inside collaboration tools (like a team chat app).

2) Self-Service Support & Service Desk

Submit support requests from the portal.

View ticket status, history, notes, attachments, and timelines.

Custom request forms with dynamic fields, templates, and required questions.

Checklists that generate tasks and sync to internal workflow systems.

CSAT / satisfaction surveys tied to tickets.

Auto-routing rules to send requests to the right queue/type/priority.

Approval workflows (manager/role-based approvals).

In-portal e-signature / acknowledgment capture when needed.

Identity verification step (support PIN or similar) before sensitive actions.

Broadcast banners and pop-ups for outages, maintenance, or announcements.

Embedded live chat for quick requests.

AI-assisted chat that can:

ask clarifying questions before creating a ticket

suggest solutions instantly

deflect simple requests

route smarter tickets to the right place

3) AI-Assisted Support & Automation

AI that learns from your internal knowledge and past tickets.

Suggested answers and steps for technicians and for clients.

Auto-drafting ticket summaries and recommended resolutions.

“Zero-touch” options for common requests (AI resolves without human help).

AI triage to improve ticket quality and reduce back-and-forth.

4) Service Catalog / Request Storefront

Organized catalog of standard services (onboarding, offboarding, access changes, device orders, etc.).

“Shopping cart” style checkout for services or products.

Portal actions trigger internal automations/provisioning flows.

Requires approvals/pricing display based on client role or plan.

5) Account Management / vCIO / Planning

Account planner for roadmaps, priorities, budgets, and QBR preparation.

Client-facing dashboards showing health, risks, progress, and recommendations.

Assessments (questionnaires) that score risk/opportunity and generate reports plus follow-up tasks.

Automated plan summaries that convert into proposals.

Archived reports library with easy upload/ingestion workflow.

6) Reporting & Insights

Infrastructure reporting (devices, servers, uptime, OS versions, warranty/age).

Cloud workspace reporting (licenses, adoption, security posture, MFA coverage).

Network reporting (site health, ISP status, performance, device inventory).

Software lifecycle reporting (end-of-life, missing updates, risky apps).

SaaS usage visibility (shadow IT and app adoption).

Training completion and compliance reporting.

Agreements/quotes/invoices visible to authorized users.

Payment portal single-sign-on integration to billing system.

Certificate/domain expiry tracking and alerts.

Exportable reports (PDF/CSV) and shareable dashboards.

7) Co-Managed IT / Intranet / Knowledge

Client intranet area with:

company links and apps

announcements

internal policies

how-to guides

Knowledge base searchable from portal.

Quick start guides for common tools/workflows.

Built-in course library for end-user training.

Role-based training assignment and completion tracking.

Daily/weekly digest summary emails of portal updates and tasks.

Company directory synced from your identity/contact source.

8) Integrations & Sync

PSA/ticketing system integration (bi-directional tickets, contacts, assets).

Identity provider integration for SSO + user sync.

Cloud workspace integration for reporting and license data.

RMM/endpoint platform integration for device health/actions.

Network monitoring integration for site dashboards.

Webhooks and workflow triggers for custom integrations.

Low-code automation connector support (trigger external flows).

9) Admin, Security, and Compliance

Tenant isolation per client.

Encrypted data storage and access logging.

Role-based access control with granular permissions.

Configurable data retention controls.

Compliance-ready agreements available when required (HIPAA/GDPR-style support).

Full white-label controls: portal name, logo, colors, login screen, email templates, and menu language.

10) Optional Security Add-Ons (if you want them in the portal)

DNS/content filtering management for clients with:

category policies

allow/block lists

safe-search enforcement

roaming/off-network protection

detailed logs and reporting

Security awareness training module surfaced directly in the portal.
